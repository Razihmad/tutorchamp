What Is a Paragraph?

When you start writing an essay, make sure the first line of every paragraph should make the point very clearly. After that, you need to expand upon the point and explain why it relevant. This is the basic step for analyzing the complete essay.

Structure Of Your Essay

The second point must be followed logically one after the other. To make your essay the best, each paragraph should take the reader to the next points. This is called signposting. These signposts give the way to keep your reader on track.

Conclude Your Argument

At last, you need to tell the audience what you are going to say, conclude it and tell them what you have stated above. With clear and logical progression, have your line of argument in the end. This will make the user have a clear understanding of the complete essay.
