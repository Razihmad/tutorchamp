No Essay ‘You Deserve It Scholarship

No Essay ‘You Deserve It’ is one of the easiest scholarships for college students. They can apply by creating an account or mailing a postcard to Sweepstakes. Mention your name, age, email address, and state of residence. If you are the winner, you can get the payment through PayPal or a wire transfer. If you're looking for scholarships for college students that are simple and easy then you must apply to this scholarship before the deadline. In this scholarship you don't have to write an essay on any topic, just fill out the form and other requirements and submit it to them.

  • Application Deadline: July 29th, 2021
  • Award : $2,222
  • Provided by : ScholarshipOwl.com
  • Draw Date : 10th August 2021.
  • Age : 16 years of age or above.

$1,000 July Scholarship

If you are a high school student or a college student then you can apply for this scholarship that is especially for college students. You can enrol in the scholarships for college freshmen by two methods, either online entry or mail entry. A new winner is selected every month so if you are not selected, you can re-apply for the scholarships to college students.

  • Application Deadline : July 20, 2021
  • Award : $1,000
  • Draw Date : 15th August 2021
  • Age : 18 years of age or above .

There's Space for Everyone Scholarship

If you are looking for another scholarship that provides you an amount with which you can improve your educational access then this Scholarship provides you with this opportunity. All the students who are undergraduate and graduate can apply for these scholarships for college students 2021.

  • Award : $2,500
  • Age : 16 years of age or above
  • Eligibility : only US residents can apply.

Feldco Scholarship

If you are an expert in essay writing then you can win these scholarships for college students. They will assign you a topic for which you have to write an essay of 700-1,000 words. You should send all your information on their email with your essay writing. If you are a legal U.S. resident and searching for scholarships for college freshmen then you must apply to this scholarship.

  • Deadline : 15th December 2021
  • Draw Date : January 7th, 2022.
  • Award : $1000

Providian Medical Scholarship.

The next one in the list of scholarships for college students in 2021 we have the Providian Medical Scholarship, which is also based on essay writing with a limit of 1500 words. This is one of the scholarships among the scholarships for college high school seniors and you must send it by using US mail. If you are one who wants to pursue his/her career in the medical field then you must apply for this scholarship.

  • Award : $500 bi-annually
  • Deadline : Jan 15th
  • Age : 16 years of age or above

Be Bold Scholarship

This scholarship is given to students who have bold profiles based on three factors, Earnest, Moving and Determined. If your profile falls under this criteria then you are eligible for this scholarship. This is given every year to lucky candidates and you should apply to it as soon as possible to get on the winner list. The candidate who applies for this scholarship must make their profile in a confident and bold way.

  • Award : $25,085
  • Deadline : July 31, 2021
  • Draw Date : November 2021

Digital Privacy Scholarship

If you want to have scholarships for college high school seniors then you must try this Digital Privacy Scholarship. This is easy to apply as you need to write a message of about 140 characters and an essay of about 500 - 1000 words. You just need to complete the form application by filling in all the details and then just send them.

  • Award : $1,000
  • Age : No limit
  • Eligibility : only US residents can apply.

Annual Financial Empowerment Scholarship

The next one is the Annual Financial Empowerment Scholarship which is all about financial empowerment, you need to create a video of about 1 minute showing the importance of financial empowerment. If you are pursuing any degree then you can definitely apply for these scholarships for college high school seniors.

  • Award : $1,500
  • Deadline : July 16, 2021
  • Draw Date : August 2021
  • Eligibility : Legal citizen of the US.

Minecraft Scholarship

This is an essay writing-based scholarship in which all the interested students are asked to write an essay on the topic '' Minecraft'' discussing the positive impact of Minecraft on their career. You must submit a 500-word essay with all the details filled in the correct manner. So if you are looking for college financial aid this scholarship is for you.

  • Award : $2,000
  • Deadline : July 31, 2021
  • Eligibility : citizen of US.

Spirit of Giving Scholarship

High school seniors as well as current college students need to write an essay of 500 - 750 words on a topic from three different topics given on their website. After you have completed the essay you need to send it to scholarship@winecountrygiftbaskets.com. They will choose 3 winners from the total students.

  • Award : $1,000
  • Winners : 3
  • Apply from : October 1, 2021
  • Deadline : July 31, 2021
  • Draw Date : Second Tuesday of August.
